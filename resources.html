Resources for Celiac Disease

  • Beyond Celiac: Education and research about celiac disease. The website includes tool kits for school administrators and nurses, and a section on living with celiac disease in college.
    (215) 325-1306
    https://www.beyondceliac.org
  • Celiac Disease Foundation: Focused on treatment and research about celiac disease. The website includes a special section on celiac disease in children, as well as comprehensive lists of gluten-free foods, products, and recipes.
    (818) 716-1513
  • FindMeGlutenFree.com: Find gluten-free friendly restaurants and reviews.
    https://www.findmeglutenfree.com
  • GIKids: The patient outreach and education arm of the North American Society for Pediatric Gastroenterology, Hepatology and Nutrition. The website offers guidance about celiac disease and the gluten-free diet.
    (215) 641-9800
    https://www.GIKids.org
  • GlutenFreeDrugs.com: Provides lists of most medications that are gluten-free (generic and brand name).
    https://www.glutenfreedrugs.com
  • Gluten Intolerance Group: Help for finding local celiac disease support groups and other resources for managing celiac disease. Special sections on the website for cooking with gluten-free ingredients.
    (253) 833-6655
    https://www.gluten.org
  • National Celiac Association: Education and advocacy organization. Free, comprehensive online course for newly diagnosed children.
    (617) 262-5422
    https://www.nationalceliac.org
  • National Foundation for Celiac Awareness (NFCA): A non-profit organization is dedicated to raising awareness of celiac disease and gluten sensitivity.
    https://www.allergyhome.org
  • University of Chicago Celiac Disease Center
    (773) 702-7593
    https://www.cureceliacdisease.org
