In the game of Snake, the player uses the arrow keys to move a "snake" around the board. As the snake finds apples (red squares), it grows larger. Unfortunately, this snake has Celiac Disease and the bread (brown squares) harm the snake and the score goes down. The game ends when the snake either moves off the screen or moves into itself. The goal is to make the snake as large as possible before that happens.
